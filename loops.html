LOOPS
current loop iteration: 4
Loop 0:
The original World. Read from the City's ruins hints that each dimension has a core, and said core connects to an "anchor", yet the Overworld itself is a dimension too. They gathered a team, both scientists and mechanists, and those who are interested about the Ancestors in general.

The results of their research pointed them towards the World's Spawn, or where the Compass points towards (x0, z0). There, they built a machine, aimed to open the core and discover the secrets within it.

Only, there's nothing else to discover with it, aside from the World Seed.
Loop 1:
The world has recreated itself, and all things seem fine. A world similar to the original one, but now the technology they need is unavailable, and resorts to stockpiling the resources they’ll need.

Slowly, however, the World Seed is expelling too much World Essence, and soon would make the World vulnerable again to the Endbringers.
Loop 2:
Someone else got to the World Seed, the World gets recreated again, Blu’s Mini- Memory Machine fails, and repurposes it to contain instructions on how to improve the World Device.

The situation will only devolve from here, until…
Loop X:
After enough loops, the world would have eroded into a Skyblock world.

After this, the World would be plunged back into the Void…
Loop X + 1:
… Except it doesn’t, with the Improvements Blu has done behind the scenes, any expended World Essence gets slowly recycled throughout loops.

This cycle has sustained itself, and will loop back over and over and over again.

Until the very last World Essence is spent.
