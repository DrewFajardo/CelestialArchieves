CELESTIAL ARCHIVES

Celestial Archives is meant to keep all records of all players involved in this ever-growing interactive story called "X-Loop." The platform itself serves as "online documents" and overviews of what the project is. It overviews past storylines and how these iterations went, how the characters play roles, and how each person's character can either formulate their own story within the X-loop's world setting or have their character join in another person's character journey throughout this world or current loop iterationâ€” but also maintaining the spirit of the plot theming of X-Loop having research or documentation that references world-building or knowing a bit more character lore. So it both shows off individual character profiles and supposedly clicks the interest of the narrative story-- despite that the general world setting is a modded Minecraft world.

The creativity of each individual is endless as long as it follows the confined rules of worldbuilding. A new batch of characters that are either the same individuals created or more people have joined in, but the current storyline needs to prepare an ending, and therefore create another loop. In this case another new storyline, either the same and additional characters or a whole set of characters!

Before X-loop had its own writing and storylines, it was initially just a server of friends playing around, and teasing about themes on each other being deities and peasants praising a potato shrine. Eventually, the Head writer and Co-Writer started building more and more about these ideas and eventually formulating multiple pages of lore, worldbuilding, and character stories. In the later year revamped the world writing and solidified the characters to be more grounded and not a character with all plot armor. Now its updated writing is separated much from the server they used to play on and now a story of its own that just so happens to take place from the initially modded Minecraft world.

Celestial Archives aims to pique the interest of those who want to take part or want to become a more introductory site for those who just want to be more on the viewer side of it. It's meant also to collate happenings on the main storylines of the world. Give viewers an overview of characters so they can also choose which character storylines they want to invest themselves in.
