ORIGINS
MERLING

They prefer to live in soggy and damp areas, especially ones that are accessible to water. Although they can perfectly live underwater just fine, they are the prime candidates to go and construct dome cities underwater. You typically won’t see them at all unless you live near the shores, but even then you’ll be lucky to see them among the rain. If you ever see one, be sure to offer them a drink, preferably a water breathing potion.

Merlings are known to be protective of their underwater cities despite the limited access to most Players. Regardless, they do tend to be defensive due to groundwalkers still making attempts to raid their cities for their treasures.

Surely, having some of the Merlings' junk be something players can fish would be enough for them to stop from raiding their homes right?
