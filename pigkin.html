ORIGINS
PIGKIN

The main berserker of the Nether. The greediest and prideful amongst all Nether citizens. With their insatiable greed, comes a good understanding of ruling over people. For if a Pigkin wants, they will stop at no means to achieve it. Making them relentless especially when hitting their bloodlust threshold which is said to be a lot lower than most. Which is also the contributor of the constant in-fighting amongst empires and towards each other.

Pigkins do not take kindly new additions to their empires if they were just given to them. Their prides see kindness, passivity, and any hint of sincerity as a sign of weakness. As such Pigkins are commonly seen as ruthless and unmerciful. Despite this facade, more often than not, the Nobles within empires are caring, enough to not sacrifice for people with no gains. That being said they do take into trading workers and fighters. But the common pursuit of these Nobles is the development of a soul weapon.

The in-fighting is a cause of sabotage to delay any and every empire in their developments and findings for a Nether Star
