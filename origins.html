ORIGINS
In the Ancestors’ pursuit of immortality, they have done a variety of experiments in order to achieve it. The very first of these experiments is done on the Physical Form, which gave rise to the various Plagues, one of which is the Undead. However, the winning run for their pursuit was when they started to experiment more on the Soul. The few experiments gave birth to the Sculk, but after much tribulation, they succeeded.

Of course, as seen by the ruins and the Undead roaming about, they didn’t care about the aftermath of all of these experiments. If they were wiped out by one, they relocated to the other, as evidenced with the numerous Sculked Ancient Cities.

One particular remnant of these soul experiments was that the essence of their species has started to rub on different lifeforms. Organic, Material, or even Conceptual. When the Ancestors have slowly died off, their descendants have witnessed a new uprising within their environment. The souls of kin have imbued themselves on different physical forms, and thus gave birth to new Origins.

Origins are remnants of the Ancestors soul essence, finding new life in a new form, their bodies augmented from the material that they took form in. As such, some instincts from the critters they’ve reflected upon are ingrained to some.
