REALMS
OVERWORLD
The “core” dimension of a world, hence the name of overworld. As such, most of the activity within the world lies in this dimension.

It’s not quite uncommon to find various creatures and cultures in this dimension, especially those who have sought refuge from their old dimensions. Being the core of a world, the Overworld is one of the oldest dimensions that serves as the anchor point of the other dimensions, as such, it has predated most of them, and may have even witnessed the birth of new ones as well.
NETHER
Notorious for the war-hungry Piglins. Although they’re now way past their prime, there have been rumors of a new Piglin uprising in the works, however this remains to be proven. With each empire being busy at being at each other’s throats on proving their wealth and superiority, the Nether is ridden with Prideful, Greedy and Wrathful citizens.

It does not make any better that with this greed is the consistent pursuit of being rich in resources as a display of power and influence, which traps the Nether in a permanent state of in-fighting and conquest invasions within the confines of the Nether.
