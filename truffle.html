TRUFFLE

Though unrelated, you may also find Truffles living amongst the Floran communities, providing a sort of symbiotic relationship between the two, with one providing food and the other providing protection at any time of day. Of course, Truffles who seek to abuse such relationships will typically find themselves dead, after all the Florans arenâ€™t weak or helpless. Being unable to ingest potion effects dictates little of their lifestyle, but it does provide them a disadvantage against the elements.

Despite them living symbiotically with Florans they're commonly cultivated as they bring good harvests with the use of their magic spores, even enhancing food properties and effects when consumed. But cross a Truffle and they won't think twice about also giving you bad harvest and bad spores on your meals.

They act as supports in enhancing the magic properties of potions too!
