ORIGINS
AVIAN

Humans have mocked this origin for the single notation, "Why in the Nether would you take the form of a dang Chicken?" However, whether the first Avians were literal chickens has been debated long since then. After all, their colorful plumes surely indicate that they are of a rather fashionable origin.

They live among mountain tops, and have an agricultural based society. It’s said that there are numerous communities of Avians around the world, and with their peaceful nature have a strong connection with the Villagers.

Although they have taken solace in the mountains, the dawn of Elytrians migrating from the End has caused a lot more Air traffic than they would’ve liked. More insultingly, them possessing extended flight has caused the mockery of Avians to intensify ever since then.
