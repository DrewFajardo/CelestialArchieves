EXCERPT
Growing up as a child soldier, Cody Pyre is a force to be reckoned with, besides her volatile and violent nature, she is a devastating combatant.

Cody Pyre is a well-known Blazeborn known for being part of the frontlines of the battles and sieges alike. Why? It seems as though this blazeborn in particular has a low threshold making her devastatingly motivated in combat. Coinciding with the fact that she relishes each battle she enters in as she is a Pigkin herself. A ruthless brute in battle but a calm and clever medic in the barracks. She is more reliant on her combat ability than heavily relying on her to treat you in battle, but she does provide great cover for any ally that needs healing and backup.

Such a great soldier comes with a troublesome and violent upbringing. She was known to be the youngest to be taken in for training, having her family killed as she was taken away by the empire troops by force. Every day she was scorned for her unwillingness to cooperate until the higher-ups decided to go about punishing those who failed or those who disobeyed. Cody was part of the latter, which made her a target for abuse amongst her peers and higher-ups. But even so every day, even on days she does decide to follow through and comply with all requests she is still punished in the most backhanded way possible, even sometimes ranging to having petty reasoning.

She doesn't miss a day when she would be poured water over her hands by her captains. Which by Blazeborn anatomy is very painful, as it's the equivalent of giving an Overworldian a second- degree burn. Due to this traumatic and painful treatment on her hands, Cody's hands have turned into the shade of coal, her skin charred, devoid of any heat and sensations from her hands. It was still functional, yes, but she felt as though it was not there.

The constant berating, abuse, and immense training have hard conditioned Cody to be cynical all throughout growing up. Only believe that she's good for battle uses. To survive in everyday life is to always fight.

One of these days she's going to snap at this everyday cycle. Maybe even cause the biggest massacres in her state of bloodlust. After all she is known to be the crimson reaper for a good reason.

EXCERPT
Willow favors his solitude more than anything-- a reserved individual who keeps a lot to himself. Commonly seen to be wearing a wooden carved mask at all times. But there would be rare instances that he would take them off around certain people or certain circumstances. Though this is just but a mere speculation.

Due to being a Floran, Willow spends a lot of his daytime walking and exploring places, never seemingly settling anywhere, a nomad. People would see him around to help where he can to ensure people are pleased and maintained throughout the day. Though can be called helpful, it is more of an innate need for Willow to make people happy or at the very least comfortable-- thus leading to his seeming overly generous attitude. But even with all that said he is not attached to anyone and just leaves without a word the next day. Seemingly off to another place to roam about. Florans rely for the most part to have sunlight as their main food source always giving Florans the energy to go around places during the day and rest or create a temporary safety hut for the night. Or at least stay above ground and live off on honey for the night if he has to continue traversing through the night.

Friendly as he appears to be, Willow doesn't hesitate to create a contingency plan with people he regularly interacts with, be it a close friend neighbour, he's passively on edge as if he's living with a knife pointed at his back constantly. He is prepared for the worst of everyone.

Not a lot is known about Willow's upbringing as he himself tends to not really talk about it, and for the most part avoids talking anything about himself. If any attempts were made he would redirect it back to the other person, shooting any attempt for any openings.

His reservation is an attempt to sever any attempt of attachments and connections and it can be seen as pointless for Willow seems to be on the go. No use in creating these memories when he'll eventually leave it all behind and never look back on it. Thus making Willow the most self- reliant person that he is. Only doing things to get by, seemingly aimlessly, or perhaps in search of something but is adamant about the sentiment of the latter.

Others would see him roam about places with a lot of Fire Resistance potions at times, but never really going to the Nether as it seems. Perhaps in touch with a Blazeborn, but no one can be for sure about it as nobody seems to know where Willow's next destination could be.
