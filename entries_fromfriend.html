ENTRY 34
from a friend
[There are small trinkets here...]



To you, the Elytrian,


I’m not sure if you’ll even be able to read this passage, nor do I know if your sanity remains intact. But seeing the circumstances you and your world has thanks to the machine you’ve repaired, I can’t say that I’m saddened by such development.


I am unable to enter your world now, as the World Seed seals your World away from the Void, and I wouldn’t want to risk entering the World with one of the End Rifts.

However, there is a brief moment that I am able to peek into your world. From the entries I’ve encountered, it seems the World Seed displaces itself from time to time.


I will help you, with the best of my abilities.

Though the entry to your world is on brief periods of rest, I will do as I can to supply and repair the Machine you were unable to repair accordingly.


From your neighborhood Merchant.
