ORIGINS
ELYTRIAN

Nimble yet fragile. Elytrians are the apex predators of the sky, being able to go head-to-head with the phantoms of the night sky. An army of Elytrians is sure to be a cause for concern, if not for the fact that there’s only a small population of them at all. Regardless, most Elytrians are peaceful, looking for refuge and start a new life away from the darkness of the End. However, they wouldn’t hesitate to attack, and are difficult to catch off guard. If one’s being pursued by one, then you might as well start digging your grave. It’s a good thing that most would rather be messengers, right?

Alternatively, Elytrians are the elite soldiers of The End hive. Thanks to their easy maneuverability within the Void, they are prime to attack and invade safe houses set up throughout the End. Unlike the Enderians, they have to make contact with the Void in order to be subjected to the hivemind, and have a variety of ways to resist the Endhive’s voice to boot. Because of the heavy resistance between the Endhive’d Elytrians and the resisting Elytrians, the population of them has slowly been dwindling out in the End.
