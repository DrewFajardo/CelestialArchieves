ORIGINS
FLORAN

Being one with the plants, their opinions on farming seem to vary. However, it’s a common sentiment that food harvest is a normal thing to undergo, and may even give a helping hand with crop farms at times. Most Floran communities that you will encounter will most likely be a pacifistic one, preferring to make peace and strike deals. However, picking a fight with them is generally a bad idea, especially if you are fighting an army in broad daylight.

Though they’re unable to properly absorb solid foods, they seem to be able to consume liquid foods just fine. Thus, you may typically find them offering you soups and stews. However, their ‘stomachs’ seem to be highly receptive to honey, so you’ll also find them with domes for beekeeping purposes. There are even some rumors about them knowing about some sort of ‘bee dimension’? What an odd way to say beekeeping domes!

Though commonly pacifistic, do not let this display fool you for they are all still capable of being armed, seemingly with farming equipment that are sharpened or bee-themed equipment such as a stinger spear.
