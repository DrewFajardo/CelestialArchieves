ABOUT X-LOOP

A world that resets over and over again, and with every reset comes a world that deteriorates. A world that will continuously retell the same story in different fonts. A world that is destined to torture its little population as some were doomed to their spawns into the void the world eventually shattering as the world tries to recreate itself over and over again with the same World seed, putting a strain on the generous usage of the World Essence. That eventually sends gigantic rifts in the Minecraft world, which ultimately gives players a view of the void below them. A destination to nothingness.

Each loop iteration, means another world reset, meaning another usage of the world essence. But in the trade of the deteriorating world, the divinities that are of the watchers thought to make this world more interesting that with every loop iteration, there is an added world condition. Such that Loop 1 the world's condition was Piglin Invasion-- and ongoing conquest of the Nether Invasion into the overworld. When Loop 2 came about there were remnants of buildings and creations of the Nether creation, but no player remembers such thing happening. In the Loop 2, the world condition was Famine. Loop 3 was surprisingly normal.

Now with Loop 4, which is where this story begins, the world condition was beserker's bloodlust. Players all have a certain threshold that builds up over time making players increasingly aggressive over time before they lose control of themselves would enter this state of mindless killing. This may include players, mobs, and whatever live creatures are in their sights, it will be killed with no thought until that state of bloodlust is satiated. Nobody knows how long the berserk can last as it seems to be inconsistent with each player, some lasting until their first kill some can lead to devastating massacres.

It seems as though all Loops have this killing mechanic that forces everyone to enter a state of fight and create a last man standing.

Found in the oldest archives of lost entries, it is believed that whoever is the last man standing from an already sparse world would ascend. But in another entry, it is to believe that the world seed is just no mere concept but the heart of the existence of the world, and can grant you to be good, hopes to rebuild the forsaken world perhaps? Or an empty plan to attain power? Who knows.
